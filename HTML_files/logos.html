Logo Concepts

Four refined, iconic marks designed for modern technology brands. Simple, memorable, and timeless—built to scale from 16px to billboard size.

Concept 01 • Primary Logo

The Growth Ring

"Layers of lasting impact"

Large
Small
Favicon 16px
Monochrome
Design Philosophy

Three concentric circles represent layers of compounding impact over time. The gradient flows from light seafoam green (new growth, hope) through turquoise (established impact) to deep blue (trust, stability). Each ring represents a phase of growth—just like tree rings mark years of life.

Why It's Iconic
  • Three perfect circles = simple, clean, balanced
  • Color gradient = journey from seed to forest
  • Concentric layers = growth over time
  • Natural metaphor meets modern execution
  • Calming, trustworthy, optimistic
Concept 02

The Horizon

"Bridging earth and sky"

Large
Small
Favicon 16px
Monochrome
Design Philosophy

Inspired by the horizon line where earth meets sky—representing the bridge between physical community impact and digital innovation. The horizontal bar divides the circular layers, creating a powerful symbol of connection. Top half (light) = transparency and hope; bottom half (deep) = rooted foundation and trust.

Why It's Iconic
  • Horizon = meeting point of two worlds (phygital)
  • Split circle = balance of opposites
  • Vertical gradient = earth to sky journey
  • Horizontal line = stability and connection
  • Instantly recognizable at any size
Concept 03

The Möbius Flow

"Endless transformation"

Large
Small
Favicon 16px
Monochrome
Design Philosophy

A Möbius strip reduced to its essence—one continuous surface with no beginning or end. The high-contrast gradient (deep emerald to rich navy) emphasizes transformation and energy. The stark white negative space cutout creates maximum depth and a bold, modern presence.

Why It's Iconic
  • Mathematical elegance meets visual power
  • Bold contrast = confident and striking
  • One continuous surface = endless cycle
  • White negative space creates drama
  • Modern, abstract, unforgettable
Concept 04

The Pulse Arc

"Waves of endless impact"

Large
Small
Favicon 16px
Monochrome
Design Philosophy

Multiple flowing arcs with bold white highlights emanate from a central point, like ripples expanding infinitely outward. The stark white accents create dramatic contrast and suggest transparency and clarity. The central white-ringed dot represents the initial donation—the stone dropped in water that creates endless waves.

Why It's Iconic
  • Ripple effect with bold white = clarity and impact
  • High contrast = confidence and visibility
  • Central origin point = clear source of change
  • White highlights = transparency and trust
  • Tech-forward data visualization aesthetic
